Updating bar-graph showing increase in rent over the past 10 years

Increase in rent in Parramatta and Sydney

From the data, we realise that the rent per week has gone up significantly both in the City of Sydney and Parramatta. 13,290 units had rent ranging from $300 - $449 per week in 2006; however, after the 2016 census, there were only 7618 units with rent ranging from $300 - $449 per week. Similarly, there were only 4162 units with a rent ranging from $450 to $ 549 in 2006; however, that has increased to 7438 units in 2016. In Parramatta, there were only 2311 units with rent ranging from $300-$449 per week in 2006; however, in 2016, the number of units in that category has increased to 10,202. There were only 150 units within the rent range of $ 450 to $ 549; however, in 2016, the number of units has increased to 8965, a 5876% increase in the number of units!

Possible reasons: After further analysis, there has been an addition of a few suburbs into the current City of Parramatta. However, despite this variable, the number of housing units with a weekly rent of $450 - $ 559 has increased dramatically.
